
The included BMP files are rgb, 8 bits per channel, no alpha channel.
They are the original versions.
They were created using Aseprite.
	https://www.aseprite.org

The included WAV files are mono, 22050 Hz, signed 16-bit PCM.
They are altered versions of the originals.
They were converted from their original format (stereo, MP3) using Audacity.
	https://www.audacityteam.org
